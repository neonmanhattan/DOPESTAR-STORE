Who We Are

Built on Passion,
Stocked with Purpose

DopeStar Store started with one simple belief: every man in Kampala deserves to look and feel his best — without breaking the bank. Founded by RIO , a Kampala native with a lifelong love of fabric, colour, and self-expression, the store opened its doors at CAPITAL CENTERs in Nabugabo and quickly became a neighbourhood staple.

What began as a small stall has grown into a full clothing destination, offering everything from everyday Knitted T-Shirts to polished blazers for the boardroom. RIO personally curates every item, sourcing from trusted suppliers across Uganda, Kenya, and beyond.

We believe that fashion is not a luxury — it's a language. And hise at DopeStar's, we help you speak it fluently.
